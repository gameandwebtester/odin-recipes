Egg Fried Rice


Fried egg rice with vegitables

here is some rice. it is delicious

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup rice
  • veggies fo your choice
  • tempeh
  • tofu
  • curry powder
  • 3 tbsp of olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the veggies
  2. Cook the Rice
  3. fry the eggs in olive oil for a couple minutes
  4. Add more oil and add the curry powder
  5. mix it all around
  6. add the tempeh and tofu
  7. heat it up a bit
  8. done
